## ✅ Pattern: Draft-to-Social Reduction Ratio
- **Source**: 2026-01-24 | Technical content analysis
- **Context**: Technical blog draft (288 lines) → Facebook post (90 lines) = 69% reduction
- **Lesson**: Expect 60-70% content reduction when converting technical/long-form to social. Focus on: core concept, 1 visual, CTA.
- **Apply when**: Repurposing blog/docs/tutorials to social platforms
